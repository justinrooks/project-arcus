# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 01/09/2026

SkyAware (“the app”) is designed to provide clear, hyper-local severe weather awareness while respecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we use, how it is handled, and what never leaves your device.

---

## Information We Collect

SkyAware does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).

- No user accounts
- No analytics
- No tracking
- No advertising identifiers

---

## Location Data

SkyAware uses your device’s **precise location** to determine local weather risk.

This location data:
- Is processed **entirely on your device**
- Is **not transmitted** to the developer
- Is **not shared** with third parties
- Is **not used** for tracking or analytics

You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.

---

## Weather Data

The app retrieves public weather information from:
- The National Weather Service (NWS)
- The Storm Prediction Center (SPC)

This data does not contain personal information.

---

## Derived Risk Levels

SkyAware computes risk-level badges and summaries based on public SPC data.  
All risk calculations occur locally on your device.

These risk levels are informational and should not be considered official forecasts or warnings.

---

## What We Do Not Do

SkyAware does **not**:
- Collect personal data
- Sell or share data
- Use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
- Transmit user data to external servers
- Process user data using AI or machine learning services

---

## Data Storage

All app data remains on your device.  
Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

---

## Children’s Privacy

SkyAware does not collect personal information and does not knowingly collect data from children.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If future features require changes to data handling, this policy will be updated and presented clearly within the app.
